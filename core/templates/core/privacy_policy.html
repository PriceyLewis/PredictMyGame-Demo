Privacy Policy

Last updated: {% now "j F Y" %}

PredictMyGrade (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and meeting our obligations under the UK GDPR.

1. Who We Are

PredictMyGrade is an educational web application that helps you estimate academic performance through data-driven analysis. The platform is designed for learners aged 16+. Anyone under 18 must obtain explicit parental/guardian consent (or school-administered consent) before creating an account or submitting any personal data.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account details (email address, username, hashed password)
  • Academic inputs (module names, grades, predictions, study plans)
  • Optional feedback or contact messages you send to us
  • Basic analytics data (pages visited, device type, time on site) captured via cookies

We do not collect special category data (e.g., health, ethnicity, financial data).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver grade predictions, study insights, and personalised dashboards
  • Improve site performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Respond to support queries, bug reports, or feedback requests
  • Monitor security, detect abuse, and maintain service integrity

We do not sell or trade your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Consent — you voluntarily provide academic data for predictions.
  • Legitimate interests — operating and improving the platform, analytics, and security.

5. Storage and Security

  • Data is stored on secure servers hosted by Render with managed PostgreSQL databases protected by TLS.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (PBKDF2) and never stored in plaintext.
  • Access is limited to authorised administrators under strict need-to-know controls and MFA.
  • Automated backups are encrypted, stored separately, and purged on a rolling basis.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data while your account remains active. You may request deletion at any time via the in-app delete flow or by contacting us at privacy@predictmygrade.com. Backups containing personal data are overwritten or anonymised within 30 days of deletion, and aggregate analytics retain only fully de-identified information.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, export, or delete your data; to withdraw consent for optional processing (analytics, marketing); to object to automated decision-making; and to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you believe your data has been mishandled. Use the in-app Privacy Dashboard to export or delete data instantly, or contact us for additional assistance.

8. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted processors who comply with UK GDPR and, where applicable, the EU–US Data Privacy Framework:

  • Render — hosting and infrastructure
  • Managed PostgreSQL providers (Render/Supabase) — encrypted database storage
  • Stripe — secure payment processing acting as our billing data processor
  • OpenAI — AI-powered study insights when you opt into premium predictions
  • Email service providers (e.g., Postmark or SendGrid) — transactional email delivery
  • Privacy-friendly analytics (e.g., Plausible) — aggregated usage insights when you consent

Each processor is bound by a data-processing agreement and may only use your information to deliver the contracted service on our behalf.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the site and optional analytics cookies to understand usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time. Learn more in our Cookie Notice.

10. Security Breach Response

If we discover a security incident that impacts your personal data, we will notify affected users without undue delay and, when required, inform the ICO or other regulators. Notifications will describe what happened, the data involved, and actionable steps you can take.

11. Young Users

PredictMyGrade is built for learners aged 16 or older. Users under 18 must provide verifiable parental or school consent before storing any personal data. We may request proof of consent and suspend accounts if we cannot validate it.

12. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact us at privacy@predictmygrade.com for any privacy questions.
